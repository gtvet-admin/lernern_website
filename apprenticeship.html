Flexi MoU is a scheme promoted by the Directorate General of Training, under the Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship. It encourages industries to collaborate with Skill Universities to provide on-the-job learning opportunities to youth who have completed 10th or 12th grade, enabling them to earn a Craftsmen Training Scheme (CTS) qualification, equivalent to an ITI certification.

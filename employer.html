Why Choose Lernern?

Most of India’s blue-collar workforce remains without formal training and certification. Less than 5% of youth in India have any formal vocational training compared to 50% in China, 70% in Germany and 96% in South Korea. With the lack of growth and upward mobility, companies see workforce volatility & high attrition rate, within a few months of hiring. This affects labour productivity & product/service quality problems. At Lernern, we aim to bridge that gap, sustainably by partnering with organizations to build technologically-adept and constantly evolving workforces.

Product Portfolio

NATS
National Apprenticeship Training Scheme

Qualifications Diploma, Graduates & Post Graduates

Duration: Upto 1 year

Governing Body: Ministry of Education supported Apprenticeship Scheme through Board of Apprenticeship Training

Subsidy of INR 4,500 per month per trainee

Status of Trainee: Apprentice under the Act, Upto 15% of workforce (25% in Maharashtra)

NAPS
National Apprenticeship Promotion Scheme

Qualifications Diploma, Graduates & Post Graduates

Duration: Upto 1 year

Governing Body: Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship supported Apprenticeship Scheme through NSDC

Subsidy of INR 1,500 per month per trainee

Status of Trainee: Apprentice under the Act, Upto 15% of workforce (25% in Maharashtra)

B.Voc / D.Voc
Embedded BVoc / DVoc

Qualifications 10th, 12th or ITI

Duration: Upto 3 year

Governing Body: Ministry of Education UGC BVoc Guidelines

Domains: Manufacturing, Electrical ,Automotive, Hospitality, Retail

Partner Universities: Centurion University, Lamrin Tech Skills University

Status of Trainee: Intern as per EPFO Guidelines, Upto 15% of workforce (25% in Maharashtra)

Flexi MoU
Flexi MoU Scheme (NCVT CTS Certifications)

Qualifications10th or 12th Pass

Duration: 2 years

Governing Body: Directorate General of Training, MSDE

Trades: Industrial Fitter, Industrial Electrician, Automotive Manufacturing, Welding

Status of Trainee: Student Trainee as per EPFO Guidelines, Upto 15% of workforce (25% in Maharashtra)

Staffing
Temp Staffing

Qualifications Any

Duration: Any

Governing Body: Ministry of Labour through region specific Labour Licenses

Statuory Compliances under all relevant acts including PF/ESI/WC etc.

Status of Worker: On Roll Contractual Employee

Who are we?

Lernern India Pvt. Ltd. (formerly Gram Tarang Technical Vocational Education & Training Pvt. Ltd.), is an upskilling, education & workforce solutions platform working towards a mission of Making Technology Enabled Learning, Education & Skills available for all, especially those excluded from formal education & training at the bottom of the pyramid.
